About Me

I am a new Full Stack Web Developer out of Centriq Training who is excited to apply what he has learned in real-world scenarios and grow to become a leader in this career. I previously worked as a Clinical Laboratory Scientist at Saint Luke's on the Plaza, but decided that I wanted a career that was more product and deadline oriented. I've always had a very creative mind and my favorite part of web dev is both coming up with bold solutions in a limited framework and having a fully functional project after all of my hard work. Creating web applications and keeping up with new technology is fascinating and I'm excited to make it my new career!
